Building Damage Heatmap

This map visualizes the damaged buildings, modeled by FEMA, throughout Houston, TX in the form of a heatmap. The damage levels included in the map are Destroyed and Major Damage. Use the slide controls to manipulate the heatmap visualization. Click on the heatmap to show a pop-up window information of the damaged buildings and level of inundation. You can search for a specific place using the search bar.

Blur Radius : 10

Max Value : 100

Min Value : 0
